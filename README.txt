CoffeShopSim is an Unreal Engine 5 (C++ & Blueprints) simulator covering various gameplay mechanics and programming topics:
	- Interacting with different kitchen appliances to cook, bake and prepare customer orders
	- Manage customer orders, including multiple items
	- Manage a queue of customers
	- AI Customers who enter the coffee shop and wait for orders
